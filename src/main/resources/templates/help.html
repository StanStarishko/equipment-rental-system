Dashboard

The Dashboard is the main overview page, displayed when you first open the application. It shows:

  • Equipment availability summary — colour-coded cards showing how many items are available, booked, or under maintenance.
  • Active bookings — a table of all current confirmed bookings with equipment name, staff member, dates and purpose.
  • Quick actions — shortcut buttons to create a new booking, add equipment, or view the full equipment list.

Equipment Management

The Equipment page allows you to view, add, edit and remove equipment items.

Searching and Filtering

Use the filter bar at the top of the Equipment page to narrow down results:

  • Search by Name — type part of the equipment name. The search is case-insensitive.
  • Category — select a category from the dropdown to show only items in that group.
  • Status — filter by availability status (Available, Booked, Maintenance, Unavailable).

Click Filter to apply or Clear to reset all filters.

Adding and Editing Equipment

Click Add Equipment to register a new item. All fields marked with an asterisk are required. Each item must be assigned to a category.

Equipment Statuses

AVAILABLE — the item is ready to be booked.

BOOKED — the item has at least one active booking. It can still be booked for non-overlapping dates.

MAINTENANCE — the item is temporarily out of service. It cannot be booked.

UNAVAILABLE — the item has been withdrawn. It cannot be booked.

Note: Equipment with active bookings cannot be deleted. Cancel all bookings first.

Booking Management

The Bookings page shows all reservations with their status, dates and assigned staff member.

Creating a Booking

To create a new booking:

  1. Click New Booking from the Bookings page or Dashboard.
  2. Select the equipment item. Items under maintenance or unavailable are shown but cannot be selected.
  3. Select the staff member responsible for the booking.
  4. Enter the start and end dates. The end date must be the same as or later than the start date.
  5. Enter the purpose of the booking.
  6. Click Create Booking.
Note: The system will check for date conflicts automatically. If the selected equipment already has a confirmed booking that overlaps with your chosen dates, the booking will be rejected and the form will retain your entered data so you can adjust the dates.

Cancelling a Booking

To cancel a booking, click the Cancel button next to the booking in the list. Only confirmed bookings can be cancelled. When a booking is cancelled, the equipment status will automatically revert to Available if no other active bookings remain for that item.

Category Management

Categories are used to group equipment items by type (for example, Audio Equipment, Computing, Photography). This makes it easier to find and filter items.

  • Each equipment item must belong to exactly one category.
  • Category names must be unique.
  • A category can only be deleted if no equipment items are currently assigned to it. Reassign or remove the equipment first.

User Management

The Users page manages staff members who can create and manage bookings. Each user has a role:

  • ADMIN — full access to all system features including user management.
  • STAFF — can create and manage bookings and view equipment.
Note: Users with active bookings cannot be deleted. Cancel their bookings first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book equipment that is already booked?

Yes, as long as the dates do not overlap. Equipment with a BOOKED status can still accept new bookings for different date ranges.

Why can I not select some equipment in the booking form?

Equipment with a status of MAINTENANCE or UNAVAILABLE is shown in the dropdown but disabled. These items must be returned to Available status before they can be booked.

What happens to the equipment status when I cancel a booking?

If the cancelled booking was the only active booking for that item, the equipment status automatically reverts to Available. If other active bookings remain, the status stays as Booked.

Why can I not delete a category?

Categories with equipment items assigned to them cannot be deleted. You need to reassign or remove all equipment from the category first.

Why can I not delete a user?

Users who have active (confirmed) bookings cannot be deleted. Cancel their bookings first, then delete the user.

What does the cost per day field mean?

This is an optional field for recording the daily hire cost of an equipment item. It is displayed in the equipment list for reference but is not used in booking calculations in the current version.
